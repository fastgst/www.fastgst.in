Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to FastGST.in. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our GST Tax Lookup API services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

About Us

FastGST.in is operated by Sangeeth Nandakumar, an independent developer. We provide GST Tax Lookup API services to help businesses and developers access accurate GST and tax information.

Information We Collect

API Usage Data

When you use our API, we collect:

  • API request logs (endpoint, timestamp, response status)
  • Usage patterns and frequency
  • Technical information (IP address, user agent)
  • Error logs for service improvement

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address for account management
  • Company/organization name (optional)
  • API key and usage quotas

Website Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand website usage patterns. This includes information about your device, browser, and general location (city/country level).

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our API services
  • Monitor API usage and enforce rate limits
  • Improve our services and fix technical issues
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements or court orders
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure API key management
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account information: Until account deletion
  • API usage logs: 12 months for service improvement
  • Analytics data: 26 months (Google Analytics standard)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications

Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for website functionality and analytics cookies to improve our services. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Payment processors for billing (if applicable)
  • Cloud hosting providers for service delivery

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
