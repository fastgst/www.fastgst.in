About FastGST.in

Building GST-compliant solutions for India's digital economy, one API at a time.

Our Story

FastGST.in was born from a simple observation: India's GST system is complex, but accessing accurate tax information shouldn't be.

The Problem

As businesses digitize, they need reliable access to GST tax rates, HSN/SAC codes, and classification data. But existing solutions were scattered, outdated, or too expensive for small businesses and developers.

We saw an opportunity to build something better - a centralized, developer-friendly API that makes GST compliance simple and accessible.

Fast & Reliable

Built for speed and accuracy, with real-time updates from official sources.
